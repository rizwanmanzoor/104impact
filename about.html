Our Mission

1O4 Impact connects capital and founders building meaningful companies across biotech, health tech, deep-tech & new energy.

We partner from seed to scale, aligning success through shared ownership and long-term relationships.

Our Approach

Aligning vision

We support purpose-driven founders in building strong, values-aligned partnerships that underpin sustainable growth. By fostering trust and mutual incentives from the outset, we help establish relationships designed for long-term success

Strategic and hands-on support

We guide ventures through every growth stage; from refining strategy and operations to scaling sustainably. Our model ensures founders have the tools and insight to execute effectively.

Joy Abiola

Founder, 104 Impact

I began my career as an analyst at Pantheon. I then moved into secondaries advisory and primary fund placements. I then joined Citi Private Bank, where I worked on the private equity team with families and institutions. I then moved into venture, where I worked with technology and growth stage companies, and where I met Dipam. This path gave me a clear view across the capital stack. Over more than a decade, I built relationships with allocators and partners across Africa, Europe, the GCC and the United States. I supported mandates across a wide range of sectors and regions, with more than one billion in total capital allocation during this period. The birth of my daughter during a time of global uncertainty shifted my focus. I became intent on building a more humane and future focused way of supporting the ecosystem.

I created 104 Impact to act as a steady partner to founders who work on systemic issues. At the core of the firm is a simple belief, that innovation should support people and planet and not take from them. I work with care and intention, aligning investors with missions that matter while keeping structure, risk and accountability in view. 104 Impact provides capital strategy, structural guidance and long term support to founder led businesses working for impact. Our strength sits in emotional intelligence, clear frameworks and the ability to connect groups that do not often meet.

My value is the ability to pair financial rigour with empathy. I know how to hold complexity, build trust and support companies not only to raise capital but to use it with clarity and responsibility. I am trusted for my judgement and for my belief in long term potential. With a family background rooted in democracy and public service, I created 104 Impact to help address the gaps that hold society back. I believe in supporting people who take on difficult problems and in building systems where responsible change can grow over time.

Dipam Patel

Dipam Patel

Co-Founder, 104 Impact

I began my career in investment banking, advising and supporting high growth technology companies through mergers, acquisitions and venture transactions. I then worked at Bryan Garnier and GP Bullhound, where I specialised in Software and Consumer Technology and oversaw more than €2 billion in completed transactions. These years gave me a detailed understanding of what supports sustainable growth, not only financial discipline but operational strength and strategic clarity. My approach to impact is grounded in deep involvement and expertise. I immerse myself in each project, relationship and venture with the aim of creating outcomes that are rooted in value. For me, impact is not defined by a sector or model but by the quality of engagement, insight and partnership I bring to the work.

In recent years, I applied this philosophy to my own ventures. This includes D2P Autoparts, an e-commerce company where I work across supplier relationships, technology systems, international reach and team development. My work there reflects my view that meaningful progress comes from being closely involved in the day to day reality of a business. As a co-founder of 104 Impact, I continue to build on this principle. I form long term partnerships, support careful decision making and help founders turn their plans into steady and durable progress. My role sits in linking strategy with practical steps and in helping businesses create value with clarity and purpose.

My background across investment banking, private markets and hands on operations shapes how I work today. I combine financial rigour with a practical understanding of how companies grow. I am trusted for my judgement and for my commitment to supporting founders over time.
