Joy Abiola

Founder

Joy’s career began in private equity before moving through secondaries advisory and fund placement into the world of venture capital, building a breadth of expertise across capital markets, fundraising and cross-border dealmaking. Along the way, she has cultivated a trusted global network of allocators spanning sovereign funds, institutions, and some of the world’s most discreet and influential family offices across Africa, Europe, the GCC and the US. She has supported over $1 billion in capital deployment across a diverse set of mandates.

The turning point came with the birth of her daughter in a time of profound global uncertainty. It sharpened Joy’s focus on what matters — building a future where people and planet are centred, and the most catalytic entrepreneurs are not just funded but truly supported.

She launched 104 Impact as the right-hand partner to founders solving real, unmet needs. The firm blends capital strategy with emotional intelligence, structure with soul, and the power of a global network with a founder-first lens. From inception to scale, 104 offers long-term strategic guidance, capital raising support, and alignment with values that drive enduring outcomes.

What sets Joy apart is her ability to move between worlds — from sovereign corridors to village cooperatives — while maintaining deep empathy, integrity and strategic clarity. In a sector often driven by speed and short-term returns, Joy builds patiently, relationally, and with a different kind of ambition.

Her work is not just to deploy capital, but to redirect its flow towards ideas that can shape society for the better.

Our Approach

More Than Capital. Embedded Partnership.

Aligning Vision and Capital

We connect purpose-driven founders with values-aligned investors to ensure both mission and returns are achieved. By fostering trust and clarity early, we create partnerships built for long-term success.

Strategic and Hands-On Support

Beyond investment, we guide ventures through every growth stage; from refining strategy and operations to scaling sustainably. Our advisory model ensures founders have the tools and insight to execute effectively.

Driving Measurable Impact

Every initiative is assessed through a clear impact lens, tracking both financial performance and social or environmental outcomes. We focus on ventures that not only grow fast but create meaningful, lasting change.

Dynamic Team With Diverse Experience

104's core team brings together deep experience across private equity, venture capital, data infrastructure, and M&A. This diversity of perspective allows us to assess opportunities through multiple lenses, unlocking insight others might miss.
