About 104 Impact

More Than Capital. Embedded Partnership.

Our Mission

104 Impact connects capital and founders building meaningful companies across biotech, health tech, deep-tech & new energy.

We partner from seed to scale, aligning success through shared ownership and long-term relationships.

Our Approach

More Than Capital. Embedded Partnership.

Aligning Vision and Capital

We connect purpose-driven founders with values-aligned investors to ensure both mission and returns are achieved. By fostering trust and clarity early, we create partnerships built for long-term success.

Strategic and Hands-On Support

Beyond investment, we guide ventures through every growth stage; from refining strategy and operations to scaling sustainably. Our advisory model ensures founders have the tools and insight to execute effectively.

Driving Measurable Impact

Every initiative is assessed through a clear impact lens, tracking both financial performance and social or environmental outcomes. We focus on ventures that not only grow fast but create meaningful, lasting change.

Dynamic Team With Diverse Experience

104's core team brings together deep experience across private equity, venture capital, data infrastructure, and M&A. This diversity of perspective allows us to assess opportunities through multiple lenses, unlocking insight others might miss.
